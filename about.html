About us

Our Mission:

At YBL Studio, our mission is to capture life's most precious moments through the art of photography. We believe that every moment is worth remembering, and that our photographs have the power to bring those memories to life. We are committed to providing our clients with exceptional service and stunning, timeless photographs that they will cherish for a lifetime.

Our Vission:

At YBL Studio, our vision is simple: to capture the beauty and magic of life through the art of photography. We believe that every moment is worth cherishing, and that photography has the power to preserve those moments for a lifetime.

Our team of talented photographers is dedicated to bringing our clients' vision to life through stunning, timeless photographs. Whether you're looking for family portraits, wedding photography, or commercial photography, we have the expertise and experience to deliver exceptional results.

Our Story

a chance beginning

YBL Studio was founded by Echo Liu and Yue, two passionate photographers who shared a vision for capturing life's most precious moments in a way that was both artistic and timeless. Their journey began with a chance encounter with a young couple searching for a wedding photographer, which sparked their passion for photography and their commitment to building a studio that would deliver exceptional service and high-quality photographs. At YBL Studio, we believe that our story is just beginning, and we invite you to join us on this journey.
